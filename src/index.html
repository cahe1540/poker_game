RULES:

Both Player 1 and the dealer are assigned 5 cards, Player 1 has the option to replace cards he doesn't want only once. When Player 1 is ready, click go. It costs 3 credits to start a new round. Earning points depends on the value of the hand. When Credits reach 0, game ends. Try for the highest possible score.

HAND COMBINATIONS IN INCREASING ORDER + Prize:
TWO OF A KIND-- Only 2 cards have the same value + 6
TWO PAIRS -- Two sets of two of a kind + 12
THREE OF A KIND--Three cards with same value + 24
STRIAGHT--All cards have consecutive values. + 48
FLUSH--All cards have same suit. +102
FULL HOUSE--1 pair and one 3 of a kind. + 205
FOUR OF A KIND--4 cards have same value. +417
STRAIGHT FLUSH--Hand is straight and flush. + 837
ROYAL FLUSH--hand contains: A, J, K, Q, 10 of same suit. + 1500

Player1 wins if he has better hand than dealer In the event of a ties, the player with the highest card in hand wins.
